Commercial Electrical Fit-Out Handover

Information a tenant, property manager, and future service electrician should receive at project closeout.

Closeout starts before construction ends

A useful handover is assembled throughout the project. Waiting until the final day makes it easy to lose product data, approved changes, and photographs of concealed routes. Assign one person to collect records while the commercial electrical work is underway.

Confirm the final scope

  • Compare the completed work with the latest drawings and change orders.
  • Identify equipment supplied by the tenant, landlord, and contractor.
  • Record dedicated circuits for kitchen, server, mechanical, and specialty loads.
  • Confirm panel directories use names understood by building staff.
  • Keep permit, inspection, and commissioning records together.

If capacity work was included, retain the basis for the panel upgrade or distribution changes. Future tenant equipment decisions should refer to the completed load information rather than assuming every spare breaker position represents spare capacity.

Lighting and control records

List fixture models, drivers, lamp types, colour temperatures, dimmers, sensors, schedules, and emergency-lighting components. For a project involving commercial lighting installation, include control instructions that staff can follow without entering programming menus unnecessarily.

Operations and maintenance

Create a recurring inspection list based on the equipment and operating environment. Staff should know how to report heat, odour, buzzing, damaged covers, moisture, or repeated tripping without attempting repairs. Plan qualified electrical safety and maintenance when conditions, usage, or tenancy change.

Handover note: Mark records as final only after approved field changes are included. Superseded drawings should remain identifiable so they are not mistaken for the installed layout.

Make the next request specific

A clear request names the building, suite, operating hours, access restrictions, affected equipment, and urgency. Start from the complete list of BGE electrical services and provide the project documents relevant to the requested visit.
